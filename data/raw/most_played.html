This is a list of the most-played video games ordered by their estimated player count, which include downloads, registered accounts, and/or monthly active users . This list does not include games with official sales figures; those are located at the list of best-selling video games page instead. This list is also not comprehensive, because player counts are not always publicly available, reliable, or up-to-date.

The list does not include mobile games , unless versions of the game have also been released on non-mobile platforms. For a list of them, see the list of most-played mobile games by player count .
